LabTracking

An initiative by the Ecosystems and Global Change Laboratory (LEMG - UFF) to store and track environmental sample data

Download Android APK

Key Features

  • 📦 Lab sample data tracking
  • 🌍 Georeferenced data collection
  • 🔥 Firebase Firestore for secure storage
  • 🔐 Google account authentication
  • 📱 Built with Flutter framework

Access Information

The Android APK is currently available only for registered users within the LEMG-UFF database.

Other research institutions can fork our open-source codebase and implement their own tracking solutions.
